Mammoth UNDERFLOOR insulation
Insulation Blanket Roll

To further reduce heat loss from your home, consider installing Mammoth's underfloor insulation product. It effectively seals the gaps between floors and the skirting boards.

Underfloor Insulation Blanket

Older homes have suspended timber floors. This product fits snuggly into the required 400m space for installation. Newer homes may have rooms above unheated spaces, such as garages. These would also benefit from installing underfloor insulation!
